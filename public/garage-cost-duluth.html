Garage cost questions

Answered.

How much does a detached garage cost in Duluth, MN?

In 2026, a standard 24×24 two-car detached garage in the Duluth area typically runs roughly $45,000 to $80,000 built to match your home — slab, framing, roofing, siding, and doors included. Larger buildings, shop space, or a loft push it up from there. The written estimate is free.

What adds the most to the price?

Size first — every added bay is slab, framing, roof, and a door. Then a finished or heated shop, a loft with stairs, extra-tall walls for a lift or RV door, and site work if the slab location needs real excavation or fill.

How long does it take?

Most detached garages are built in 4 to 8 weeks once the slab is in. A straightforward 24×24 lands at the short end of that range.

Do you handle the permit and the slab?

Yes — it’s one job with one contractor. ProCon pours the slab, pulls the permit, and runs everything through to the doors, licensed in Minnesota under #QB807406 and insured.
