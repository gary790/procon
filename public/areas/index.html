Why a tight radius

Local isn't a
tagline here.

We stay close to home for a reason. A builder who works one region knows things a regional outfit can't — how the frost line dictates a footing here, which assemblies actually hold heat through a Northern Minnesota January, how a hillside lot drains, how the wind comes off Superior in one neighborhood and not the next.

That knowledge is the difference between a house that fits its site and one that fights it. It only comes from building the same ground, season after season — which is exactly what we've done since 1997.
