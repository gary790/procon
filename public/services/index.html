One builder,
every trade.

Whatever the project, the arrangement is the same: Dan Bruckelmyer quotes it, plans it, and is on site while it's built. No handoff to a crew you never met, no project manager between you and the person making decisions. It's how Pro Con has worked since 1997.

And it's all backed the same way too — licensed in Minnesota under #QB807406, insured, and finished with a workmanship guarantee, whether it's an 8-month custom home or a 2-week deck.
